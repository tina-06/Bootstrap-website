Panchmarhi

Pachmarhi is a hill station in Hoshangabad district of Madhya Pradesh state of central India. It has been the location of a cantonment since British Raj. It is widely known as Satpura ki Rani, situated at a height of 1067 m in a valley of the Satpura Range in Hoshangabad district.
